About SGBIT Alumni

The SGBIT alumni establishes a strong and lasting relationship with its passed out students and harness their skill and expertise in their respective domain to guide the students in their early stage of their engineering. The alumni students who hold prominent positions in the industry can guide the students by providing the knowledge about the best practices in industry, provide internship and job opportunities. Further, they can visit their almameter and give lectures on diverse subjects. The institute organalizes alumni day every year to bring the memories of the past and welcome suggestions from the alumni and further improve the stature of the institute. All suggestions which would bring overall improvement in the functioning of the institute and enhance the quality of teaching learning and also the enhance the placement opportunities of the students currently studying in the institute.

Alumni Objectives

  • Foster better ties with Alumni Students
  • Seek valuable inputs to improve the Quality of Education
  • Create internship and job opportuinites for the students
  • Organize Lectures and workshops to educate the faculty and students
  • Improve the Industry Institute Interaction
